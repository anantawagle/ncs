About Nepal Computer Suppliers (NCS)


Welcome to Nepal Computer Suppliers (NCS), your reliable source for cutting-edge computing solutions in Nepal. We are more than just a technology vendor; we're your trusted partner dedicated to providing top-quality products and exceptional customer service.


Our Story


Founded in 2023, NCS has evolved from a small computer shop to a leading provider of laptops, desktops, printers, and accessories in Nepal. Our journey began with a vision to bridge the technology gap, empowering individuals and businesses with high-quality and affordable computing solutions.


Our Commitment


At NCS, we are committed to delivering the best value to our customers. We take pride in offering a curated selection of products from renowned brands, ensuring you have access to the latest technology that meets your needs. Our commitment extends beyond just selling products; it's about building lasting relationships with our customers.
